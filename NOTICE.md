# Notice

APV2 is published as a source-available public research preview. The official
release repositories are intended for reading, local reproduction, technical
review, academic discussion, and non-commercial research evaluation.

The project is not released under an OSI-approved open-source license. The code,
documentation, generated reports, experiment traces, skill packages, curricula,
and release artifacts are governed by the APV2 Public Research License in
[LICENSE](LICENSE).

The project contains provenance metadata, release-lineage markers, manifests,
hashes, and audit helpers used to preserve authorship continuity and support
reproducible review. These records do not alter AP cognition, do not watermark
generated outputs, and do not execute hidden behavior.

The main research direction is called **Endogenous Cognitivism / 内生认知主义**:
cognition is treated as an endogenous information-flow closure among
perception, state-field competition, attention, memory, cognitive feeling,
emotion, action, and feedback.
