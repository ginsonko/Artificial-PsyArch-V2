## 中文版

### 一、一个今天的 AI 还没正面回答的问题

这几年，大模型的能力有目共睹：写文章、写代码、答疑、调用工具，样样都行。但如果你和它连续相处一整天，会发现一件奇怪的事——它其实并不“一直是它自己”。

每开一轮新对话，它常常要把前面发生的事重新读一遍才能接上；记忆往往是外挂的数据库，用的时候检索回来，不用就搁在一边。这更像一台机器每次开机都重新装载记忆，而不是一个始终在场、连续活着的心智。

APV2 研究的，正是这个被绕过的底层问题：**一个系统怎样才能在连续的时间里，始终是同一个“它自己”？** 它不和大模型对立，而是想补上大模型较少触及的那一层。

### 二、AP 是什么：不是更大的记忆，而是一个“一直亮着的当前心智场”

APV2（下称 AP）的核心想法，可以用一句话概括：**把“想、看、说、改、记”都放进同一个一直运转的循环里，并且每一步都看得见。**

它的做法有几个让人印象深刻的特点：

**1. 它有一个“当前状态场”，而不是一段对话历史。**
现实输入、刚刚的念头、过往经验、对接下来的预测、心里的压力、行动的倾向、行动之后的后果——这些都不是日志，而是同时摆在一张“桌面”上、彼此竞争注意力的活对象。AP 给每个对象标了两种能量：一种是“现在真的在场”，一种是“正期待它出现”，两者的差就是“认知压力”。这让“现实”和“预期”始终分得清，却又能相互拉扯。

**2. 它的“感觉”不是装出来的，而是从内部过程里长出来的。**
很多系统会说“我不太确定”“我应该重试”，但那往往只是一种说话风格。AP 不一样：它的不确定、违和、证据不足、疲劳、闭合感，全部由内部的预测错配、能量张力等过程量生成，并且能真正进入记忆、影响下一步的注意和行动。换句话说，**它的“犹豫”是有据可查的，不是表演。**

**3. 它会自己纠正错误，而不是把错误记成“钢印”。**
当 AP 反复预测错一个东西，这种“错配”会产生负向的认知压力，把这个错误联想推开；而正确的经验几乎不受影响。我们在实验里看到，一个错误联想的相似度从 +0.36 被推到 −0.59，而正确联想几乎纹丝不动。这意味着它能“吃一堑长一智”，又不会因此误伤已经学对的东西。

**4. 它分得清“接下来”和“是同一类”。**
“B 常常接在 A 后面”（顺序）和“A 和 B 是同一类东西”（相似）是两回事。AP 把它们放在两条独立的通道里：训练“A→B”的顺序时，后继强度从 0 升到 0.94，而 A 和 B 到底像不像，分数纹丝不动。这让它能学会模仿和接话，却不会把两个不相干的词当成近义词。

### 三、凭什么相信它：四层证据，层层独立

一个新架构最容易被质疑的是“你是不是只在一个精心挑选的例子上能跑”。AP 的回应方式是把证据摆成**四个互相独立的层次**（见配图一）：

- **第一层·机制本身**：底层循环在 16 种参数扰动下都保持同样的定性行为；短期记忆的顺序是“软偏好”而非“硬规则”；被打断后能恢复；预测有清晰的“下一拍”节奏；记忆能存盘再读回。最新还补上了在线学习向量的三项消融，分别证明它“有用、会纠错、不越界”。
- **第二层·和真实大模型同台对照**：在一个“规则未知、只能靠反馈慢慢学”的任务上，AP 路线用 **0 个 token、0 次外部调用**达到满分；真实的 Claude（无记忆）只有约 0.22–0.30；而配了记忆和工具的真实 GPT 拿到 0.83–0.85（见配图二）。我们特意没有用弱模型当靶子——强对手拿到高分，恰恰说明这是**机制、成本与可审计性的差异**，而不是田忌赛马。
- **第三层·任务能力**：一套 AP-Core 关键能力测试 8 项全过；过程锚点实验显示，AP 学到的“认知感受”能跨越不同表面任务迁移，而伪造的“假感受”会失败。
- **第四层·第三方独立复现**：另一位作者用完全不同的语言（Rust）独立重写了 AP 的核心机制，跑通了全部 8 条命令、84 项测试——说明这套结构能跨语言、跨工程被重建，不是某一份代码的偶然。

这四层里，任何一层都可以单独检验。**它们越独立，整体就越难被一句“运气好”推翻。**

### 四、它的价值和意义

AP 的价值，不在于它现在就比大模型“更聪明”，而在于它认真对待了一个被冷落的问题：**持续、可解释、能自我纠正的心智，应该长什么样。**

- 对普通人，它给出一个不同的想象：AI 不一定只能是“每次重新读一遍上下文的超级问答机”，也可以是一个**始终在场、会犹豫、会改正、会记住自己**的连续存在。
- 对研究者和工程师，它给出一个罕见的组合：**全程白箱、可审计、可消融、可被第三方复现**——在一个个人/小团队原型里，这已经接近能达到的最高标准。
- 对整个领域，它是一条互补的路线：不取代大模型，而是把“一个系统如何持续地成为它自己”这件事，变成可以动手测量的工程问题。

### 五、边界（我们想说清楚的话）

我们对它的主张是积极的，也是有分寸的。AP 现在证明的是**底层认知机制的成立**，不是开放世界里成熟的中文对话能力——那条线（GL 教学路线）仍在推进中。它的全部能力来自“状态—预测—行动—反馈—记忆”的内生闭环，不依赖答案表、不靠关键词硬匹配、不藏外部求解器、学生端不偷接大模型。把边界说清楚，不是为了示弱，而是为了让前面那些证据，**每一句都立得住**。

## English Version

### 1. A question today's AI still hasn't answered head-on

Large models can write, code, answer questions, and call tools. Yet spend a full day with one and something feels off: it isn't really *the same self* throughout. Each new session it often has to re-read what happened before; its memory is usually an external database, fetched when needed and shelved otherwise. That is closer to a machine reloading its memory at every boot than to a mind that stays continuously present.

APV2 studies exactly this bypassed question: **how can a system remain one continuous “self” over time?** It does not oppose large models; it aims to fill in a layer they rarely touch.

### 2. What AP is: not bigger memory, but an always-on “current mind field”

The core idea of APV2 (“AP”) is one sentence: **put thinking, seeing, speaking, revising, and remembering into one always-running loop, and keep every step visible.**

Four features stand out:

**1. It has a current state field, not a chat history.** Inputs, the thought just had, past experience, predictions, internal pressure, action tendencies, and consequences all sit on one “desktop” as live objects competing for attention. Each carries two energies — *really present now* and *expected to appear* — whose gap is “cognitive pressure.” Reality and expectation stay distinguishable yet in tension.

**2. Its “feelings” are grown from internal process, not faked.** Many systems say “I’m unsure” as a style. AP’s uncertainty, dissonance, evidence gaps, fatigue, and closure are generated by internal prediction mismatch and energy tension, and genuinely enter memory and shape the next step. **Its hesitation is auditable, not performance.**

**3. It corrects its own errors instead of stamping them in.** When AP repeatedly mispredicts something, that mismatch creates negative pressure that pushes the wrong association away — while correct experience is barely touched. In our experiment a wrong association’s similarity was driven from +0.36 to −0.59, while the correct one hardly moved.

**4. It separates “what comes next” from “what is the same kind.”** Succession (“B follows A”) and similarity (“A and B are alike”) live on separate channels. Training “A→B” raised successor strength from 0 to 0.94 while the A–B similarity stayed put. It learns to imitate and continue without conflating unrelated words.

### 3. Why trust it: four independent layers of evidence

The easiest attack on a new architecture is “it only works on one cherry-picked example.” AP answers by stacking **four mutually independent layers** (see Figure 1):

- **Layer 1 — the mechanism itself:** the loop keeps the same qualitative behavior under 16 parameter perturbations; short-term order is a soft bias, not a hard rule; it recovers after interruption; prediction has a clear next-beat rhythm; memory persists and reloads. Three new ablations show the online learned vector is useful, self-correcting, and bounded.
- **Layer 2 — head-to-head with real large models:** on an unknown-rule, feedback-only task, the AP route scores a perfect 1.00 at **zero tokens and zero external calls**; real Claude (no memory) reaches only ~0.22–0.30; real GPT with memory and tools reaches 0.83–0.85 (Figure 2). We deliberately avoided a weak strawman — a strong opponent scoring high is the point: this is a difference of **mechanism, cost, and auditability.**
- **Layer 3 — task ability:** a core AP capability suite passes 8/8; process-anchor experiments show AP’s learned “feelings” transfer across different surface tasks, while fabricated “fake feelings” fail.
- **Layer 4 — third-party reproduction:** another author independently rebuilt AP’s core mechanisms in a different language (Rust), passing all 8 commands and 84 tests — evidence the design is reconstructable across languages and codebases.

Each layer can be checked on its own. **The more independent they are, the harder it is to dismiss the whole as luck.**

### 4. Why it matters

AP’s value is not that it is already “smarter” than large models, but that it takes seriously a neglected question: **what should a continuous, explainable, self-correcting mind look like?**

- For the general reader: AI need not be only a brilliant question-answerer that re-reads its context each time; it can be a **continuously present** entity that hesitates, corrects itself, and remembers who it is.
- For researchers and engineers: a rare combination — **fully white-box, auditable, ablatable, and third-party reproducible** — which, for a small-team prototype, is near the highest realistic bar.
- For the field: a complementary route that turns “how a system stays itself over time” into a measurable engineering problem rather than a slogan.

### 5. Boundaries (what we want to be clear about)

Our claims are positive and bounded. AP currently demonstrates **the soundness of its underlying cognitive mechanism**, not a mature open-world Chinese dialogue ability — that line (the GL teaching route) is still in progress. All of its ability comes from an endogenous state–prediction–action–feedback–memory loop: no answer tables, no keyword hard-matching, no hidden external solver, and no student-side large model. Stating the boundary is not a sign of weakness; it is what lets every claim above stand.
